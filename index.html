The Vision: Where This Is Heading

Phase 1: Simple Form (Now)

Start with a simple Google Form to capture your skills and interests. Quick and easy to get involved.

Phase 2: Burner Profile System (3-6 months)

Your profile becomes part of Undiscovered Events - the platform already used by BOSS and other NSW events for ticketing and event management.

Phase 3: Integrated Volunteer Management (6-12 months)

Event organisers can search, contact, and roster volunteers directly through the platform. Streamlined coordination and communication.

Phase 4: Community Hub (12+ months)

Your Burner profile becomes your identity across all NSW events - with communication tools, project collaboration, and community building features.

Common Questions

Why should I trust this with my information?

Built by community members, for community members. Your data stays in the community - never sold or commercialized. Transparent governance by appointed Community Data Stewards ensures your interests come first.

What if I'm too busy to volunteer much?

You control your availability and commitment level. Get contacted only for opportunities that match your interests and schedule. Start small and grow your involvement over time.

I'm new to the community - is this for me?

Absolutely! This is the perfect way to get connected and find your place. Mentorship opportunities are available, and all experience levels are welcome.

How is this different from Facebook groups?

Facebook groups are great for discussion, but they're not designed for skills matching and career development. The Burner Profile system lets you specify your interests and get contacted for relevant opportunities, rather than hoping you see the right post at the right time.
