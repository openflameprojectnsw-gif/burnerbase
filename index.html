Our Solution: The Burnerbase

A volunteer skills database that connects experienced community members with events that need their expertise.

For Volunteers

  • Create your skills profile
  • Choose which events can contact you
  • Get matched with relevant opportunities
  • Build your Burns community involvement

For Organisers

  • Find volunteers with specific skills
  • Access the whole community, not just your network
  • Build stronger, more capable teams
  • Reduce recruitment time and effort

Common Questions

Is this free to use?

Yes, completely free. This is a community initiative, not a commercial product.

Who can see my information?

Only event organisers you specifically approve. You have full control over your privacy settings.

What if I change my mind?

You can update your privacy settings, pause your profile, or delete your account anytime.

How is this different from just posting in Facebook groups?

The Burnerbase lets organisers search for specific skills and experience levels, making it much easier to find the right person for each role.
