Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

The simple version

  • We only collect information you choose to share
  • You control who can see your information
  • We never sell your data to anyone
  • You can change your privacy settings or delete your account anytime
  • Community Data Stewards oversee how we handle your information

What information we collect

Information you give us:

  • Name and contact details
  • Skills and experience
  • Availability and preferences
  • Accessibility needs
  • Event participation history

Information we create:

  • When you last updated your profile
  • Which events have viewed your profile
  • Communication logs (for safety and accountability)

Information we don't collect:

  • Financial information
  • Detailed personal history
  • Information from other websites or social media
  • Location tracking or device information

How we use your information

To connect you with opportunities:

  • Show your profile to events you've approved
  • Send you relevant volunteer opportunities
  • Help organisers find volunteers with specific skills

To improve the service:

  • Understand which skills are most needed
  • Identify gaps in volunteer coverage
  • Make the platform easier to use

To keep everyone safe:

  • Verify volunteer experience claims when needed
  • Investigate complaints or safety concerns
  • Maintain records for accountability

Who can see your information

Event organisers you approve:

  • Can see the information you choose to share
  • Can contact you directly about opportunities
  • Must follow our Code of Conduct when contacting you

Community Data Stewards:

  • Oversee data governance and privacy compliance
  • Can access information to investigate complaints
  • Are bound by strict confidentiality agreements

Technical partners (Undiscovered Events):

  • Provide secure hosting and technical infrastructure
  • Cannot access or use your data for commercial purposes
  • Are bound by a Data Processing Agreement

We never share your information with:

  • Marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Government agencies (unless legally required)
  • Anyone else not listed above

Your privacy controls

You can:

  • Choose which events can see your profile
  • Select what information to share with each event
  • Update your privacy settings anytime
  • Download all your information
  • Delete your account completely

You can't:

  • See other volunteers' private information
  • Contact other volunteers directly through our platform
  • Access event organiser contact lists

How we protect your information

Technical security:

  • Encrypted data storage and transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access controls for staff
  • Secure backup and recovery systems

Governance security:

  • Community Data Stewards provide oversight
  • Regular privacy impact assessments
  • Clear policies for data access and use
  • Transparent reporting on data practices

Your rights under Australian law

Under the Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Complain about privacy breaches
  • Have your information deleted in most circumstances

Data retention

We keep your information:

  • While your account is active
  • For 12 months after you delete your account (for safety and accountability)
  • Longer if required by law or for legitimate safety concerns

We delete your information:

  • When you request account deletion (after 12 month safety period)
  • If your account is inactive for 3+ years
  • When no longer needed for the purposes collected
