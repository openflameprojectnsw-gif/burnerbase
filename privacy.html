1. Information We Collect

üìù Profile Information

What we collect:

Basic Details

  • Name and contact information
  • Location (general area, not specific address)
  • Preferred communication methods
  • How you heard about us

Community Information

  • Burns experience and events attended
  • Skills and talents you want to share
  • Availability and time commitment
  • Community goals and interests

Why we collect it: To match you with relevant opportunities and help event organisers find the right people for their teams.

üîß Technical Information

What we collect:

  • Basic website usage data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Device type and browser information
  • IP address (for security and analytics)
  • Form completion and interaction data

Why we collect it: To improve the platform, ensure security, and understand how people use the system.

‚ùå What We DON'T Collect

  • Sensitive personal data: No health, financial, or government ID information
  • Social media tracking: No cross-platform tracking or social media integration
  • Unnecessary data: We only collect what's needed for volunteer coordination
  • Third-party data: We don't buy or receive data about you from other sources

2. How We Use Your Information

üéØ Primary Uses

  • Skills matching: Connect you with relevant opportunities
  • Event coordination: Help organisers find suitable volunteers
  • Communication: Send you relevant opportunities and updates
  • Platform improvement: Understand usage patterns and improve features

üìä Community Analytics

  • Aggregate reporting: Anonymous statistics about community skills and interests
  • Platform metrics: Usage data to improve the system
  • Community insights: Trends to help event organisers plan better
  • Research: Anonymous data for community development research

‚úÖ Legal Basis (Australian Privacy Act)

We process your information based on:

  • Legitimate interests: Community coordination and volunteer matching
  • Community benefit: Improving NSW Burns events and community connections

5. How We Protect Your Information

üîê Technical Security

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access controls: Multi-factor authentication for administrators
  • Regular backups: Secure, encrypted backup systems
  • Security monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for threats and breaches
  • Regular updates: Security patches and system updates

üë• Operational Security

  • Limited access: Only authorised personnel can access data
  • Audit trails: All data access is logged and monitored
  • Training: Regular security training for all team members
  • Incident response: Clear procedures for security incidents
  • Regular reviews: Quarterly security assessments

üö® If Something Goes Wrong
