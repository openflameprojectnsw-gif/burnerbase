Join the Burnerbase

Create your volunteer profile and help NSW Burns events find the right people for the right roles.

What you'll do:

  • Share your skills and experience (about 10 minutes)
  • Choose which events can see your profile
  • Get contacted directly for relevant opportunities
  • Help build stronger, more inclusive events

Before you start:

  • Read our Privacy Policy to understand how we protect your information
  • Review our Code of Conduct for community guidelines
  • Have about 10 minutes to complete the form

What happens next?

  1. Form submission: Your information is securely stored
  2. Profile review: We'll verify your submission (usually within 48 hours)
  3. Welcome email: You'll receive confirmation and next steps
  4. Event matching: Organisers can find you for relevant opportunities
  5. Stay updated: We'll contact you about opportunities that match your skills

Questions?

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is important to us:

  • You control who can see your information
  • Community Data Stewards oversee data governance
  • You can update or delete your profile anytime
  • We never sell your data to anyone
