üìã What We'll Ask You

Before you start, here's a preview of the 9 sections in your Burner profile:

1. Basic Information (2 minutes)

  • Name, email, location
  • Preferred contact methods
  • How you heard about us

2. Burns Experience (1 minute)

  • Experience level (newcomer to veteran)
  • Events you've attended
  • Years in the community

3. How You Participate (2 minutes)

  • Ways you've contributed (volunteering, art, organizing)
  • Roles you've held
  • Theme camps or groups you've been part of

4. Skills & Talents (2 minutes)

  • Technical skills (sound, lighting, construction)
  • Creative abilities (art, music, performance)
  • Organizational skills (planning, coordination, safety)
  • Other talents you'd like to share

5. Availability & Commitment (1 minute)

  • Time you can commit
  • Preferred scheduling (weekends, weekdays, flexible)
  • Travel willingness

6. Your Community Goals (1 minute)

  • Roles you want to grow into
  • Skills you want to develop
  • How you want to be more involved
  • What would make volunteering more rewarding

7. Volunteer Experience (1 minute)

  • Previous volunteer satisfaction
  • Challenges you've faced
  • What support you need

8. Accessibility & Support (30 seconds)

  • Physical accessibility needs
  • Communication preferences
  • Support requirements

9. Privacy & Permissions (30 seconds)

  • Which events can see your profile
  • Contact preferences
  • Data sharing choices
