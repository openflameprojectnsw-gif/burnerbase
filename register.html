Your Burner Career Development

This form is designed to help you chart your path in the NSW Burns community. We'll ask about your current experience, skills you want to develop, and how you want to grow your involvement.

Based on Proven Community Research

This form uses the same structure as our successful 2025 NSW Burns Community Survey, with categories and questions that our community understands and responds to.

üìã What's Included in Your Profile:

Profile Sections:

  • Basic Info: Contact details and location
  • Burns Experience: Your history and involvement level
  • Skills & Talents: What you bring to the community
  • Career Goals: Where you want to grow
  • Availability: Time commitment and scheduling

Career Development:

  • Role Aspirations: Leadership, technical, creative paths
  • Skill Development: What you want to learn
  • Community Impact: How you want to contribute
  • Networking: Who you want to connect with
  • Privacy Settings: Control who can contact you

What Happens Next?

1. Profile Review

We'll review your profile to ensure it's complete and follows our community guidelines. This usually takes 1-2 business days.

2. System Integration

Your profile gets added to the searchable database that approved event organisers can access.

3. Opportunity Matching

Event organisers search for volunteers with your skills and experience for relevant opportunities.

4. Direct Contact

You get contacted directly about opportunities that match your profile, interests, and career goals.
