For Event Organisers

Want to access the Burnerbase for your event?

We're currently working with Third Degree and Sunset Town organisers to pilot the system. If you organise NSW Burns events and want to be involved:

  • Email: organisers@openflameproject.org
  • Include your event name and how you'd like to use the Burnerbase
  • We'll get back to you about joining the pilot program

Technical Issues

Website or form not working?

Email: tech@openflameproject.org

Include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Your device and browser type
  • Any error messages you saw

Response Times

We aim to respond within:

  • General enquiries: 2-3 business days
  • Technical issues: 1-2 business days
  • Privacy/data requests: 48 hours
  • Code of conduct reports: 48 hours
  • Urgent safety issues: Same day

Emergency? If someone is in immediate danger, call 000. Don't wait for email responses.

About Our Team

The Open Flame Project is run by passionate NSW Burns community members who volunteer their time and expertise. We're committed to:

  • Radical transparency in our operations
  • Community-first decision making
  • Privacy and data protection
  • Inclusive, accessible design

We're not a formal organisation (yet) - we're a group of community members working together to solve coordination challenges through technology.
