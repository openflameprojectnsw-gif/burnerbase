For Event Organisers

Want to Access the Burner Profile System for Your Event?

We're currently working with Third Degree and Sunset Town organisers to pilot the system. If you organise NSW Burns events and want to be involved:

  • Email us: open.flame.project.nsw@gmail.com
  • Include: Your event name and how you'd like to use the Burner Profile system
  • Tell us: What volunteer coordination challenges you're facing
  • We'll respond: With information about joining the pilot program

What Event Organisers Get:

  • Search volunteers by specific skills and experience
  • Contact people directly for relevant roles
  • Access to the broader NSW Burns community
  • Streamlined volunteer coordination
  • Integration with Undiscovered Events platform
  • Professional volunteer management tools
  • Community-owned data and governance
  • Support from experienced organisers

About Our Team

Community-Driven Initiative

The Open Flame Project is run by passionate NSW Burns community members who volunteer their time and expertise. We're committed to:

Our Values:

  • Radical transparency in our operations
  • Community-first decision making
  • Privacy and data protection
  • Inclusive, accessible design

Our Approach:

  • Built by burners, for burners
  • Open source principles and values
  • Collaborative development process
  • Community feedback and iteration

We're not a formal organisation (yet) - we're a group of community members working together to solve coordination challenges through technology and community building.

Partnership with Undiscovered Events

Technical Partnership

Ryan (T-Dog) from Undiscovered Events is generously supporting this initiative by building the Burner Profile system into his existing platform. This partnership provides:

Technical Benefits:

  • Professional-grade platform and tools
  • Integration with existing event systems
  • Local Australian support and development
  • Regular updates based on user feedback

Community Benefits:

  • Community ownership of data and governance
  • No commercial exploitation of volunteer information
  • Transparent decision-making processes
  • Focus on community building over profit
