Community Voices: Why We Need the Burnerbase

These quotes are from the 2025 NSW Burns Community Survey, representing 518+ community responses.

Event Organisers: The Challenge

"Workload generally falls on a few key people, on site camp members can be flaky"
— Event Organiser
"An easy way for volunteers to sign up for shifts without me having to contact them all"
— Team Lead
"Role clarity, understanding what roles need what skills"
— Volunteer Coordinator
"Better system to allow volunteers to collaborate earlier"
— Department Head
"Clarity of communication especially deadlines and requirements - need to be listed clearly in one document"
— Event Organiser

Event Organisers: The Vision

"At Sunset Town, we're always looking for people with specific technical skills - sound engineers, lighting technicians, safety coordinators. The Open Flame Project would help us tap into the community's expertise instead of scrambling to find the right people through personal networks."
— Shane SOS, Sunset Town Organiser
"Third Degree has always prided itself on strong community participation, but coordinating volunteers remains one of our biggest challenges. The Open Flame Project addresses a real need - we often know there are skilled people in the community, but we don't have an efficient way to connect with them."
— Dale Mathews, Third Degree Organiser

Community Members: Ready to Contribute

"These events are transformational and I wish to share that gift with others"
— Experienced Volunteer
"Living as an example of how you want to be in life, how you want to show up in community"
— Community Member
"Being able to be myself and drop the mask I have to wear in a neurotypical world and not be judged"
— Neurodivergent Community Member
"I am a DJ with a multiverse of personas & they can all come out to play"
— Creative Community Member

Community Members: Seeking Connection

"I feel a bit disconnected living in the country, I'm hard to know when other events are on"
— Regional Community Member
"More guidance for new burners and information on how to be involved"
— New Community Member
"Training for bigger roles"
— Aspiring Volunteer
"More clarity on other people's roles, clarity somewhat on my own role"
— Current Volunteer

Theme Camp Leaders: Collaboration Success

"We've shared a large solar array with other camps and that went brilliantly"
— Theme Camp Organiser
"Group resources, how about if we have a technologist, artist and or engineering genius at hand"
— Theme Camp Lead
"Our camp has done bar take-overs and music take-overs with other camps. It has always been a great success"
— Collaborative Camp Organiser

The Community Vision

"Unified community that grows, trusts, and supports each other"
— Survey Respondent
"A space for learning, unlearning, releasing, connecting & reconnecting in community that is encouraging, giving and accountable to one another"
— Community Visionary
"An inclusive community event with space for everyone, that is priced reasonably so low income people can come"
— Accessibility Advocate

What This Means

These voices show our community has:

  • Abundant skills ready to be shared
  • Coordination challenges that technology can solve
  • Desire for connection across event boundaries
  • Vision for collaboration that benefits everyone

The Burnerbase turns these challenges into opportunities by connecting the right people with the right roles at the right time.
