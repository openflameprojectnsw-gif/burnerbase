About the Burner Profile System

The Burner Profile system is designed to help you advance your career in the NSW Burns community while connecting you with opportunities that match your skills and interests.

Your Career Development Platform

Think of it as LinkedIn for NSW Burners, but focused entirely on community building and personal growth. Instead of hoping the right opportunities find you, you can actively shape your involvement in the community.

How It Works

1. Create Your Profile

Tell us about your skills, experience, and what you want to achieve in the Burns community. Include your career aspirations and areas you want to grow.

2. Set Your Preferences

Choose which events can contact you, what types of roles interest you, and how much time you want to commit.

3. Get Matched

Event organisers search for people with specific skills and experience. You get contacted directly for relevant opportunities.

4. Build Your Career

Take on roles that develop your skills, expand your network, and advance your involvement in the community.

The Evolution: Where We're Heading

Phase 1: Simple Form (Now)

Start with a Google Form to capture your skills and interests. Quick and easy to get involved.

Phase 2: Burner Profile System (3-6 months)

Your profile becomes part of Undiscovered Events platform with advanced search and matching capabilities.

Phase 3: Integrated Management (6-12 months)

Event organisers can search, contact, and roster volunteers directly. Streamlined coordination and communication tools.

Phase 4: Community Hub (12+ months)

Your Burner profile becomes your identity across all NSW events with project collaboration, mentorship programs, and community building features.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Facebook groups?

Facebook groups are great for discussion, but they're not designed for career development and skills matching. The Burner Profile system lets you specify your goals and get contacted for opportunities that advance your involvement, rather than hoping you see the right post at the right time.

What if I'm not sure what I want to do?

That's perfect! The system includes career development questions to help you explore different areas of interest. You can start with small roles and gradually discover what excites you most.

Will this replace personal connections?

Not at all! The goal is to supplement personal networks, not replace them. Many of the best opportunities still come through relationships, but this system ensures those relationships can form based on shared interests and complementary skills.

What about privacy and data security?

Your privacy is our top priority. You control exactly which events can see your information and contact you. We follow Australian privacy laws and maintain transparent governance through Community Data Stewards.
