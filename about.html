About the Burnerbase

The Burnerbase is a volunteer skills database for the NSW Burns community. It helps connect experienced community members with events that need their skills.

How it works

For Volunteers:

  1. Create your profile with your skills and experience
  2. Choose which events can see your profile
  3. Get contacted directly for opportunities that match your skills
  4. Update your availability anytime

For Event Organisers:

  1. Search for volunteers with specific skills
  2. See experience levels and past contributions
  3. Contact volunteers directly
  4. Build better teams faster

Why we built this

Our 2025 community survey showed:

  • 425+ people ready to volunteer
  • 180+ prepared for leadership roles

What makes us different

Community owned

Your data belongs to the community, not a company. Community Data Stewards oversee how we handle your information.

Privacy first

You control who sees your information. Only events you approve can contact you.

Skills focused

Match people to roles based on experience, not just availability.

Cross-event

Connect with opportunities across all NSW Burns events.

Getting started

  1. Create your profile - Tell us about your skills and experience
  2. Set your privacy - Choose which events can contact you
  3. Stay updated - We'll let you know about relevant opportunities
  4. Get involved - Connect with events that need your skills

Ready to join? Creating your Burnerbase profile takes about 10 minutes and helps organisers find the right volunteers for the right roles.
